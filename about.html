About

Rockholt Communications exists to make the “basic internet plumbing” boring again: domains, DNS, email, and simple websites that keep working quietly.

Our philosophy

  • Clarity: you own your domain and your credentials.
  • Security-first: safe defaults, minimal attack surface.
  • Maintainability: fewer moving parts beats fancy dashboards.
  • Transparency: clear scope, clear pricing, clear handoff.

How we work

We start by mapping what you have (domain registrar, DNS, email provider, old hosting), then we move one layer at a time—email and DNS validated before any legacy systems are retired.

This site is intentionally lightweight and hosted on Cloudflare Pages for speed, reliability, and low maintenance.
